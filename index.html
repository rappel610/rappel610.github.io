Digital Account Opening Demo

A solo, end-to-end simulation of the account-opening flow used by real Banking-as-a-Service platforms. About me

I'm an Associate Software Engineer at MSU Federal Credit Union, where I build and support the API and webhook integrations connecting a Banking-as-a-Service platform serving 400,000+ credit union members and 50,000+ university students to vendors like Plaid, Middesk, MoneyKit, Kinective, Socure, and SendGrid. My day-to-day is C#, ASP.NET Core, SQL Server, and Azure, plus the customer-facing side of tracing a failure across internal code, vendor platforms, and client requests, then explaining the fix to engineers and non-technical stakeholders alike.

I came to engineering from a non-traditional path: retail management, then teaching English in Japan, then Hack Reactor. I still build things end-to-end on my own time, like the account-opening demo above, to keep sharpening that instinct from an empty repo to a working product.
